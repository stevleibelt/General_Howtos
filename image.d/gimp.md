# Gimp

## Plugins

Finding the path on your system is easy.

* Open GIMP
* Click on `Edit` (German: `Bearbeiten`)
* Click on `Settings` (German: `Einstellungen`)
* Click on `Folders` (German: `Ordner`)
* Click on `Plugins`

The first shown path is typically your users private plugin path.  
Place in all your plugins inside this and restart gimp.
